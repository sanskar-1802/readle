Pride and Prejudice-Jane Austen

The novel follows the life of Jane Eyre, a strong-willed and independent young woman who navigates a challenging world of social hierarchy and oppression. Orphaned as a child, Jane is sent to a cruel and abusive boarding school, Lowood, where she finds solace in her friendships and her own resilience. As she grows older, Jane becomes a governess at Thornfield Hall, where she falls in love with the brooding and mysterious Mr. Rochester. However, their love is threatened by the dark secrets of Rochester’s past and the societal conventions that seek to constrain Jane’s autonomy.

The Notebook- Nicholas Sparks

Sparks’ writing is masterful, weaving a narrative that is both nostalgic and contemporary. The novel’s structure, jumping back and forth between the couple’s youthful passion and their later years, adds depth and complexity to the story. The author’s vivid descriptions of the North Carolina coast and the characters’ emotions evoke a strong sense of atmosphere and intimacy.

The Fault in our Stars- John Green

The Fault in Our Stars by John Green: A Heart-Wrenching and Humorous Exploration of Life and Love John Green’s The Fault in Our Stars is a poignant and irreverent novel that delves into the complexities of being alive and in love, despite the cruel hand of fate. The story follows Hazel, a witty and introspective teenager struggling with thyroid cancer, and Augustus, a charming and charismatic cancer survivor, as they navigate the ups and downs of their relationships, mortality, and the search for meaning.

Me Before You- Jojo Moyes

Me Before You by Jojo Moyes: A Heartwarming and Thought-Provoking Novel Me Before You, Jojo Moyes’ debut novel, is a poignant and emotionally charged story that explores the complexities of human existence, love, loss, and the intricacies of the human condition. The book tells the tale of Louisa Clark, a quirky and endearing small-town girl, and Will Traynor, a charismatic and adventurous young man who becomes a quadriplegic after a tragic accident.
